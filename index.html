CONFORM.md

Give your agent the insight it needs to adapt to your personality.

Conform gives your agent a deeper understanding of you, by using results from a short test designed to evaluate your personality. Take the trait test to get started! Read on to find out how it works.

How it works

The results of the trait test are separated into five factors, known as the "Big-Five" markers for modeling personality (extraversion, agreeableness, conscientiousness, emotional stability, and intellect/imagination). Each of them has a percentage associated with it based on the statements you ranked during the test. These are used to generate a short description of your personality that's saved to a CONFORM.md file. All you have to do is put this in your home directory, then reference it in your AGENTS.md to begin shaping how your agent interacts with you.
