Conform

Make any agent your agent, by giving it the insight of knowing more about you.

Conform helps your agent form a deeper understanding of your personality. You start by answering a set of questions designed to evaluate personal traits.
The results are separated into five factors: extraversion, agreeableness, conscientiousness, emotional stability, and intellect/imagination.
Each of these will have a percentage associated with it, which are used to write a short description of your personality.
