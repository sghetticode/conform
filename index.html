CONFORM.md

Give your agent the insight it needs to adapt to your personality.

Conform gives your agent a deeper understanding of you, by using results from questions designed to evaluate your personality. Take the trait test to get started! Read on to find out how it works.

How it works

The results of the trait test are separated into five factors: extraversion, agreeableness, conscientiousness, emotional stability, and intellect/imagination (known as the Big-Five markers for modeling personality). Each of these will have a percentage associated with it, which is used to write a short description of you. This is saved, along with instructions, to a CONFORM.md file that you put in your home directory. Then you can reference it in any AGENTS.md to shape how your agent interacts with you based on your personality.
