Conform

Give your agent the insight it needs to adapt to your personality.

Conform helps your agent form a deeper understanding of your personality. You start by answering a set of questions designed to evaluate personal traits.

Take the trait test

The results are separated into five factors: extraversion, agreeableness, conscientiousness, emotional stability, and intellect/imagination. Each of these will have a percentage associated with it, which is used to write a short description of you. This is saved to a CONFORM.md file that you put in your home directory. It's meant to be referenced in your AGENTS.md to adapt the responses of an agent to your personality.
