Conform

Make any agent your agent, by giving it the insight of knowing more about you.

Take the trait test

Conform helps your agent form a deeper understanding of your personality. You start by answering a set of questions designed to evaluate personal traits. The results are separated into five factors: extraversion, agreeableness, conscientiousness, emotional stability, and intellect/imagination. Each of these will have a percentage associated with it, which is used to write a short description of you. This is saved to a CONFORM.md file that you put in your home directory and then referenced in your AGENTS.md to adapt the responses of an agent to your personality.
